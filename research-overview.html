Research overview

SignLab was founded with the general mission to pursue new foundations for scientific inquiry in sign language research and develop practical applications for sign language users and learners, leveraging recent developments in artificial intelligence, computer vision and computer graphics. Research in the lab revolves around four pillars:

  • Methods
  • Data
  • Theory
  • Applications
Use the drop-down menu in the navigation bar to learn more about each of these pillars.
