New methods

Data collection methods

We have built a state-of-the-art motion capturelaboratory, where we obtain precise 3D measurements of the movements of signers. We have also developed a new method to obtain fine-grained 3D measurements of the signer's facial expressions using depth-cameras. Finally, we have developed several new experimental elicitation paradigms to obtain controlled data on specific constructions in sign languages.

Data analysis

We use machine learning techniques (e.g., clustering) to analyse data obtained with motion capture equipment and depth-cameras. We are also developing a standard for the annotation of non-manual elements in sign language data, and methods to assess the reliability of such annotations.

Visualization

Drawing on recent developments in computer graphics and animation (e.g., virtual humans) we are developing new methods to visualise our measurements and the results of our data analyses.
